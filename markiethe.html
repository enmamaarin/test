About Us

Marikeithe & Co is developing an app to help you reserve custom jewelry products. With our app, you can design a unique piece of jewelry for your loved one and easily make a reservation for your custom order. We make it simple for customers to create and reserve personalized jewelry, tailored just for them.

Purpose

Our goal is to offer high-quality custom jewelry at fair prices. Marikeithe & Co is a small, local business that takes orders through our Facebook page. The Marikeithe & Co Reservation App is designed to make it easier for customers to reserve custom jewelry. With the app, orders can be organized by reservation, helping us keep track of each customer's individual order more efficiently.
